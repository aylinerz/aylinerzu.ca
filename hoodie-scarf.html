The Hoodie-Scarf

Goal

OCAD University, The Yonge Street Mission - 29 Toronto Design students and 5 Muslim immigrant women from different countries partnered to unite their skills and innovative thinking for a mutual goal. Their ultimate aim was to give new immigrants means to integrate to the society while proudly contributing to their family’s financial bottom line.

Goal

OCAD University, The Yonge Street Mission - 29 Toronto Design students and 5 Muslim immigrant women from different countries partnered to unite their skills and innovative thinking for a mutual goal. Their ultimate aim was to give new immigrants means to integrate to the society while proudly contributing to their family’s financial bottom line.

Process

Eventually, I decided to go with easy to sew inexpensive materials and especially focused on things that are fast to produce. Additionally this product had to target the young segment (that is spending the most), and had to be somewhat close to their Middle Eastern culture so that they would feel comfortable to produce and sell it as part of their brand.

Exhibition

I got the opportunity to display my work at the OCAD U X YSM Regent Park Exhibition and the Toronto Offsite Design Festival. I was also awarded the 'Love Tag' by DesignLines Magazine
