Hi! I'm Aylin.

I believe my passion to create new designs, fix things and come up with ideas is what led me to OCAD. I am a passionate 4th year Industrial Design student. I am always learning new skills and refining my current skills as I am a believer that there is always room for improvement. I like to focus my work on things that would ease our everyday life; no matter what I design - be it wearable technologies, simple household tools, soft goods or websites- I always make sure to have it functional, creative and user centric. My main goal is to have people enjoy my creations.

When creating new things, I like experimenting with different materials such as; woodworking, pottery, plastic molding and many others. I always find it motivating to combine my creativity with my technical knowledge. I am also known to be enthusiastic about exploring new tools/applications to further expand my knowledge. Through my school projects I gained experience in Photoshop, Rhino and AutoCAD. I love the field I work in and enjoy the challenges each project brings. I’m very ambitious and aim to be an asset in whatever team I am in.

When I am not busy learning or working on projects, you can likely find me re-watching HIMYM or The Office for what I am guessing is the 4th time. I will never miss a Marvel movie and you can always count on me to have a book with me. Now that you know a little about me, come check out my Portfolio and Let’s Chat.
